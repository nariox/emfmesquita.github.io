About Edson

For the past 8 years, I have been working on companies where I could build myself as a software engineer while being in really stimulant environments. That allowed me being passionate, motivated, thrive under pressure while learning new things.

Aside from that, I'm always working on parallel projects (some of them you can find here), since programming not only is my job but also my hobby and passion.

Some of my best features lay on system integration and the ability of fast learning, which allows me to learn new technologies and existing systems as well.

I’ve graduated in electrical engineering with computer emphasis by USP (Universidade de São Paulo) after receiving two full scholarships one at Colégio Bandeirantes (High School) and another at ISMART (granted for gifted students with low income) and have always reached for opportunities to improve my skills as a developer.
